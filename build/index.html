I'm a User Experience and Graphic Designer based in Stockholm, Sweden. Since 2004 I have been helping clients and companies building useful and efficient products and interfaces.

What I Do

I provide pragmatic and functional design, based on real user and business needs.

  • User Experience Design is the transformation of user needs and business goals into products and applications. This work starts with studying and learning and is followed by sketching, prototyping and testing different solutions. Close contact with clients and end-users is the key to a successful and consistent design philosophy.

  • Graphic Design goes hand in hand with good functionality. A consistent look and feel that corresponds to the logical structure of a system helps users understand how things work. Graphics and type are vessels for the user experience that dictate legibility and trust.

  • Frontend Design is the process of securing quality of interactions and graphics. As a designer it is essential to work with production code (or as close as possible) to minimize the gap between design decisions and the reality of implementation. HTML, CSS and JavaScript in its many shapes and forms are some of my favorite tools.
